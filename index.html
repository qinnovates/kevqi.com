The Journey

Middle School

The Flashlight

Cassette tape case + battery + wires + switch + bulb. My first build. Ordinary components, wired together, producing light.

2003–2024

7 SIEMs

Built security infrastructure from scratch at every company. Chromocell → Freedom Mortgage → HarperCollins → Blackstone → Tinder → Match Group. Same pattern: identify signals, classify, detect, alert.

2025–2026

QIF Framework

161 attack techniques against brain-computer interfaces. NISS severity scoring. Neural Sensory Protocol. The first open threat catalog for neurotechnology.

qinnovate.com →
2026

SixDots

A braille learning app that teaches through haptic feedback. 100+ Swift files. Category-based sonification (novel IP). Braille Beat rhythm game. Multi-language braille on mobile.

To build BCI output channels, I first needed to understand how humans decode haptic information. Braille is the oldest, most refined haptic encoding system in existence.

2026

Spot

LiDAR guide mode for vision-impaired dog owners. Stairs feel like ascending taps. Curbs thump. Walls buzz. Your dog's position pulses through your hand.

Most blind people will never have a guide dog. 1–5 year waitlist. $20–50K. Your pet dog already knows you. Spot helps you see them.

Next

BrainSIEM

EEG pattern classification. ADHD vs non-ADHD spectral signatures. The same SIEM methodology, applied to the brain. My 8th build.

Future

Neurosecurity Appliance

A secured brain-computer interface. User thinks a letter. Phone vibrates it in braille. User reads their own thoughts through their fingertips. The whole channel is secured.
